About Pixel

The Computer Science and Engineering Department of JNTU Anantapur plays host to PIXEL organized in the month of December. Started in 2005 as an effort to promote undergraduate-level research, PIXEL today spans over a wide vista of events that give students an opportunity to hone their skills. The annual technical festival provides an excellent platform for budding Computer Science engineers from across the country to showcase their ideas and learn from industrial exposure and of course, we award you for the best performances....

About Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering has started functioning in the year 1987-88. The department has Excellent computing facilities with necessary software. The department has right blend of experienced and young & dynamic faculty members. Within a short time the department has developed comprehensively and established as a center of excellence.
The department is striving hard to train the students adequately so as to enable them to face challenges in the industry. The Department has the tradition of conducting this symposium every year. This is the Eighth consecutive year that we are going to conduct this event on a grand scale.

About JNTU

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur (JNTU Anantapur) is a state university in Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh, India. Founded in 1946,
JNT University Anantapur started its functioning. As a budding University it is committed to develop and nurture a Technical Education System in the four districts of Rayalaseema viz., Anantapur, Chittoor, YSR, Kurnool districts and SPSR Nellore district which would produce skilled man power of high quality comparable to the best in the world and in adequate numbers to meet the complex technological needs of the economy. The University has initiated many schemes to assure quality education to the students, career development of faculty members, progression of students towards higher education and generating career opportunities through the campus placement programmes in reputed organizations across the globe.
